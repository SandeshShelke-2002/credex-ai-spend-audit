# Economics

Assumption:
Average converted Credex customer value = $1000.

If 1 in 10 consultations converts:
- 10 consultations = 1 customer

Assume:
- 100 audits
- 10 consultation bookings
- 1 conversion

Revenue:
$1000 per 100 audits.

CAC:
Mostly organic traffic, estimated near $0 initially.

To reach $1M ARR:
Need approximately 1000 customers annually at similar economics.

Key funnel:
Audit -> consultation -> credit purchase
